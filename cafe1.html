We make all our food fresh daily in our kitchens “from scratch” using the best ingredients such as local eggs & produce, extra virgin olive oil, french chocolate, imported olives & cheeses, garden fresh herbs such as flat leaf parsley, tarragon, rosemary & oregano. We marinate and roast our angus roast beef & carne asada, farm fresh-raised turkey & chicken and bake many of our own specialty items.

Our chili, soups, sauces, salad dressings, spreads are unique and proprietary. We do this so that we can deliver the "homemade quality & taste" that is the hallmark of LA Cafe!  And because we love you, our customers!

We are also featuring many GLUTEN FREE and VEGAN options on our new menu including the famous IMPOSSIBLE burger. This is a 100% Vegan Burger. Can't wait for you to try it and let us know what you think. 

Please be advised that LA Cafe is not a Vegan, Vegetarian or Gluten Free establishment. All our products are prepared and cooked in the same kitchen using same utensils and equipment.
